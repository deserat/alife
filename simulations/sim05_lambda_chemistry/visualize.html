Key Findings

THREE PATHS, SAME FAILURE
Echo (Holland's CAS model), chemical organizations (COT/Vasas), and AlChemy (lambda calculus) ALL fail at multi-scale composition — each from a different starting point (CAS theory, origin-of-life chemistry, computational theory). This convergence is strong evidence the composition problem is fundamental, not an artifact of any single approach.
